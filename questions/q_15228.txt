Plagiarism: Submission of material that is wholly or substantially identical to that created or published by another person or persons, without adequate credit notations indicating the authorship. Forgery: Forgery, alteration, or misuse of any Institute document relating to the academic status of the Student. Distortion: Any act that distorts or could distort grades or other academic records.
