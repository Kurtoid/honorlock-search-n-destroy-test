What relationship is used in the following passage? 10. Events leading to alcohol abuse in the United States took place in the eighteenth century. The most profitable way to do this was to convert the extra grain into something Easterners would buy: whiskey. As a result, whiskey production rose. And the increase in whiskey led to an increase in its use. Before long, alcohol consumption in the country rose to alarming proportions.
