Ekugcineni kuba nemiphumela emibi kubantu, ezilwaneni nasezitshalweni. Ukuguquka kwesimo sezulu kunomthelela kulokhu kushoda kwamanzi. Konke okusemhlabeni kudinga amanzi ukuze impilo iqhubeke. Ingxenye enkulu yomzimba womuntu yakhiwe ngamanzi. Ukungawatholi amanzi anele kwenza umuntu ancishelwe ('dehydrated') amanzi emzimbeni. Lokhu kudala izifo kanye nokufa. Uma imvula ingani, imithombo yamanzi ('reservoir') efana namadamu nemifula iyoma bese kuba nesomiso. Amazinga aphezulu okushisa amunca amanzi asemhlabathini. Konke okutshaliwe okungusizo kuyafa noma kungamili kahle. Ukufa kwezitshalo kunomthelela wokuphela kwendalo kanti impilo incike kokutshaliwe. Isizukulwane singagcina singazazi ezinye zezihlahla zendabuko.     2.   Izilwane zidinga ukuphuza amanzi ukuze ziphile. Izinhlanzi nezingwenya zisebenzisa amanzi njengamakhaya azo. Ukuphela kwamanzi asetshenziswa izilwane kwenza isibalo sazo sinciphe. Izilwane ezikhiqiza ubisi nenyama nazo ziyathinteka bese kuba nomthelela omubi kubantu. Izinkampani ezinkulu nezincane ziyawasebenzisa amanzi. Kukhona amabhizinisi angavalwa uma amanzi engekho abantu baphelelwe imisebenzi. Izibhedlela zona zithinteka kakhulu ngoba amanzi ahambisana nezempilo. Ukudla, iziphuzo nokunye kuyakhuphuka ngenxa yokuthi imali eningi isetshenziselwa izindlela zokuthola amanzi. Izithuthi nemidlalo esebenzisa amanzi iyaphazamiseka uma eshoda. Abagwedli, abatshuzi, abadobi nababhukudi baphelelwa umdlandla, isimo sempilo yabo siyashintsha. Imincintiswano yasemanzini ingamiswa ngoba yenziwa khona kuphela. Lokhu kwehlisa izinga labantu abasafisa ukungenela le midlalo.     3.
